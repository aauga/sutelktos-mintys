Privacy Policy

This privacy policy defines how your personal data is collected, used, and stored when you use the website https://sutelktosmintys.lt (hereinafter – the "website") and register for the offered activities.
‍
1. Data Collection
1.1. The following data is collected in the contact form:  
- Child's age;
- Chosen activity plan;  
- Chosen activity;  
- Your (adult person's) full name;  
- Email address;  
- Phone number (optional);  
- Additional information (optional) – a free-form field to provide any extra requests or remarks.
1.2. This data is collected to evaluate the registration request, contact you, and provide appropriate services.

2. Legal Basis for Data Processing
2.1. Data is processed based on your consent (Article 6(1)(a) of the GDPR). By submitting data in the contact form, you agree to its processing for the purposes described in this policy.

3. Data Storage
3.1. Your data is stored for up to 1 year from the last contact, unless a different storage period is required by law.  
3.2. All data is stored in a secure environment and is not transferred to third parties without lawful basis.

4. Child's Age Data
4.1. The website collects only the child's age information provided by an adult (parent, guardian, or legal representative) and uses it solely to provide appropriate activity offers.  
4.2. No other child data is collected.

5. Cookies
5.1. Only necessary technical cookies required for proper website operation are used.  
5.2. No cookies are used for analytics, marketing, or user tracking purposes.

6. Your Rights
6.1. You have the right to access your data, request correction or deletion, object to processing, or request data portability.  
6.2. To exercise your rights, contact: info@sutelktosmintys.lt

7. Contacts
If you have questions or comments regarding the privacy policy, contact: info@sutelktosmintys.lt
