Fun activities for curious little learners

Meet our activities, where we create a safe and engaging space for children. Here they learn to focus, express emotions and gain self-confidence through experiential activities.

🧠

Focus and Attention Development

During these sessions, we will develop children's emotional intelligence, self-control, and self-regulation through play and creative activities. A programme tailored to your child will help them develop the habits they need to overcome various challenges, manage their emotions and focus on a single activity.

📕

Homework Support (up to 5th grade)

These sessions offer personalized guidance for children up to 5th grade, helping them complete homework with confidence and deepen their understanding of school subjects. In a friendly and supportive environment, they strengthen their confidence in their abilities and learn to ask for help when they need it.

📚

Learning to Read

We guide children step by step—from recognizing letters and sounds to understanding sentences. Through playful activities, games, and storytelling, we spark curiosity and help them grow into confident, joyful readers.
